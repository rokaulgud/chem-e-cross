Instructions

Solving the Crossword

  • Click a cell on the crossword grid, or click a clue to enter your guess in the grid.
  • Click twice on a cell to toggle between across and down.
  • The active cell is highlighted in blue
  • Start typing your guess and hit enter when you are done typing to move on to the next word.
  • The word will turn green or red if you get it right or wrong.
  • You can use the tab and shift-tab keys and the arrow keys, to move around the crossword.
  • Your progress is saved in real-time, so you can close the browser window at any time and revisit the crossword later. Do not clear cookies or cache and use the same device and browser if you wish to do so.
  • For the best experience, view the site on desktop.

Rules and Regulations

Download
  1. Chem-E-Cross will be a four-crossword puzzle series with one crossword releasing each in the months of November, December, January and February.
  2. Chem-E-Cross is an individual participation competition. Undergraduate college students from any engineering background can participate in this competition.
  3. You have to submit the correct answers obtained from the crossword through an electronic form (Google form/MS form) using the submit button before 11:00 P.M. IST.
  4. Timestamp of the submission will be considered in case of a tie.
  5. Your mobile number will be considered as your unique ID throughout the series.
  6. The answer to each of the given clues is unique. The answers are case sensitive, so use only lower-case letters to fill your answers in the electronic form.
  7. Any form of plagiarism using social media or other means will lead to immediate disqualification.
  8. The final decision-making authority lies with Team AZeotropy, IIT Bombay.

Leaderboard

  1. A leaderboard will be maintained with the scores of all participating students for every month.
  2. Your score of this round will be compounded with those in forthcoming crosswords, and there will be an overall leaderboard apart from an individual monthly leaderboard.
  3. The leaderboard will be shared within a week of the date of completion of each edition with all the participants.
  4. The overall leaderboard will feature only those students who participate in all four puzzles of Chem-E-Cross series.

Certificates and Prizes

  1. Top 3 winners of the monthly crossword will be awarded prizes and certificates.
  2. Top 5 winners of the overall Chem-E-Cross series will be awarded special prizes and top 10 participants will be awarded certificates.
  3. Top 10 winners of the monthly puzzle will feature on our social media handles.
