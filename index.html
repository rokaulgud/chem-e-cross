Announcement

The March edition of the crossword series was live on Sunday, 14 March 2021. This was the final round for this year, and winners will be chosen based on their aggregated score in all four rounds. Thank you for your enthusiastic participation throughout the competition duration! We are glad that we could offer a fun and educational puzzle each month and help you kill some time during the lockdowns. AZeotropy 2021 was held virtually for the first time ever on 20-21 March, and was a huge success with over 10,000+ participants. Owing to your welcome response during the debut year of the competition, Chem-e-Cross has now found a permanent place in AZeotropy's roster of diverse and interesting competitions.

Cheers!
Rohit, on behalf of Team AZeotropy 2021
